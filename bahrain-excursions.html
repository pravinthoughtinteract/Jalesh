The pearly kingdom of the Gulf

Bahrain provides a great introduction to the Arab culture, traditions and nature, alongside impressive architectural masterpieces. Discover a traditional souk or learn more about the famous sport involving the ship of the desert and make every moment an unforgettable experience with a plethora of avenues to explore.

FLAVORS OF BAHRAIN (BAH01)

Your Bahrain excursion starts off with the spectacle of Bahrain’s architectural heritage at the impressive Sheikh Salman bin Ahmed Al-Fateh Fort. A splendid view of an ancient date palm tree farm and the Hunanaiya valley followed by Camel Farm visit let you revel in the true essence of Bahrain. Savour your lunch at the Naseef Restaurant, ask the Chef for secret recipes, while you gorge on a delicious scoop of Bahrain’s exotic ice cream. We suppose a visit to Bahrain International Circuit and the Forumla 1 race track Welcome Centre is bound to be the highlight of your trip. What’s more, you get to immerse in the culture of Bahrain at its capital market city - Souk Al Manama, get insights on Islamic religion at the Al Fateh Grand Mosque, and access to the history of an ancient Bahrain civilization at the National Museum.

PASSIONATE INSIGHTS OF BAHRAIN (BAH02)

The passion involved in crafting this excursion lets you discover the passion of what Bahrain has to showcase to its visitors. Explore the alluring architectural grandeur of the largest mosque in Bahrain, Al Fateh Grand Mosque. Experience the traditions of the Bahrain National Museum halls and ancient burial mounds. Following which, a visit to the Bahrain Fort lets you travel back in time to witness the history of its 5000 year-old civilization – Dilmun. A glimpse of one-of-a-kind Formula 1 Bahrain International Circuit and visiting their official merchandising shop is the cherry on top of your travel experience.

THE PRIDE & GLORY OF BAHRAIN (BAH03)

Discover the prestige of Bahrain’s noble achievements at the Military Museum, the first of its kind in the Gulf. Broaden your horizons about Oil and Gas production enroute to the world of speed at the F1 Bahrain International Circuit where you’re offered a behind-the-scenes tour to learn about all the celebrities who have won or lost in Formula One. A visit to nearby Camel Farm will surely immerse you in admiration for the proud ships of the desert.

SNAPSHOTS OF BAHRAIN (BAH04)

Your excursion presents you with a unique opportunity to capture the essence of Bahrain’s best historical and modern sites through timeless snapshots. Bahrain’s renowned Al Fateh Grand Mosque and the Diplomatic Area eagerly await to leave their mark of impression on you. Once you immerse in the grandeur of The National Museum & the National Theatre of the Kingdom, you’ll know that they are amongst the crown jewels of the city. You also get to witness the unique Wind Turbines at the World Trade Centre building and Manama’s spectacular skyline. A sneak peek of the ancient Burial Mounds and the F1 Bahrain International Circuit alongside picture-perfect moments of camels relaxing in the sun at the Royal Camel Farm is going to be a long lasting memory.

SOUK, GOLD, MALL & BELLY DANCE (BAH05)

History beckons you with a warm smile at the Bab Al Bahrain at Customs Square, former central business district. Indulge in the historical heritage of old Bahrain bazaars at the entrance of Manama Souq which retails a variety of speciality gold, pearls and diamonds. Enjoy an enriching shopping experience at the iconic World Trade Centre, while you also get to witness the promenade with a unique view of the H-shaped Four Season Hotel. What’s more you can also celebrate an Arabic-Lebanese lunch at Muju, while an eastern belly dance promises to entertain your indulgences. Your excursion is inclusive of a buffet lunch, a glass of red or white wine, soft drinks and much more.
