Entertainment

When it comes to entertainment on the high seas, it’s a full steam ahead. From extravaganza plays, breath-taking magic shows, live music concerts, dance shows, shopping to a host of other entertaining shows that will keep you surprised throughout at cruise.

The Broadway Experience

We don’t want you to simply watch a show, we want you to experience it. With a brand new show, specially designed for the Jalesh Cruises guest, we will have you singing along and be a part of the action from overture to final bows. World-class, international performers are ready to blow you away and have you hungry for more. This is a spectacle not to be missed!

The Burlesque Experience

Looking to add a little spice to your night? Stay up late with our world-class performers and see them in a whole new light. Pushing the boundaries is an understatement in this provocative, adults-only burlesque performance. Come experience the boldest performance at sea.

The Dome

If music and a vibrant nightlife is more your scene, we have DJ sets to fit every mood. Our resident DJ will keep parties lively until it’s time to go late in The Dome with our premier DJ who will have your dance hits covered nightly. Whatever your preference we have a venue to match your style. Premium bar service, great music, good times.

Movies

Don’t want to miss out on the all-time favourite blockbuster movie? Check out our big screen movie selection. We have daily showings of the much-loved film releases across a wide variety of genres. Matinee, evening and late-night shows are available on the high seas. Come for the movies, stay for the popcorn.
