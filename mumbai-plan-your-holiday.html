MUMBAI

Mumbai lies on the Konkan coast on the west coast of India and has a deep natural harbour. It is home to three UNESCO World Heritage Sites: the Elephanta Caves, Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, and the city's distinctive ensemble of Victorian and Art Deco buildings.

Welcome to the city of dreams

Discover the most happening and lively places of Mumbai and unleash the beauty of the city that never sleeps. Spend a day in Mumbai by visiting the Elephanta Caves, the Marvels of the Mumbai, bow your respect to the religious abode and watch the sunset overlooking the Queen’s necklace followed by scrumptious cuisine. Mumbai’s Spice market is surely a delicious treat to your senses. Visit Banganga & Kotachiwadi Heritage, local market town and meet the Dabbawallas and end your tour by walking the paths of Dhobi Ghat.
