Deck Plans

Experience living the cruising life wrapped with Indian hospitality, exotic culinary, thrilling adventure activities and amazing shore excursions. Clear sky above, blue sea below, this cruising will bring each day something new to do. Step onboard to experience luxury layered with royalty, elite and exclusivity.

Deck 12

Plantation Restaurant

A casual self-service dining venue and all meals are included in your fare. The breakfast, lunch and dinner menus include a huge range of hot and cold dishes similar to the meals served in the Waterfront Restaurant.

Jain Haven

The dedicated Jain Cuisine service whips up an array of authentic Jain dishes for Breakfast/Lunch & Dinner. There’s also a special pav bhaji counter for those who want to indulge in their favourite street food

THE INTERNATIONAL GRILL

Dig into a selection of Gourmet chicken, fish & vegetable burgers. Make your very own Pizzas. Indulge in the comfort of simple homemade pastas. Served all day long, this station is a must experience.

CHOPSTIX

Take your taste buds on a culinary tour as we bring the best of Thai, Malay, Chinese, Korean, Mangolian, Taiwanese & Japanese delicacies all at a one place.

The Cafe

Calling all chocoholics! When you are craving a chocolate fix while onboard a visit to The Cafe will hit the spot. Try the warm chocolate volcano with a melting heart of pure chocolate or the freshly baked Belgian waffles, served with chocolate sauce of course. Open from early in the morning until late at night and located on deck 12 right near the pool, The Cafe is the perfect spot to grab a quick snack, coffee or cocktail. 

Lido Pool

Two swimming pools and bar.

Deck 14

Health Spa & Salon

Has the goods to loosen those muscles, relieve that tension and clear that mind (charges apply). We’re not just talking facials and massages. Reflexology, acupuncture, men’s treatments – even smile whitening. And if you’re not feeling quite prepped for the poolside, we’ve got you covered with spray-tans, body scrubs, and the full service range of a hair and beauty salon. Open daily, though we suggest you make arrangements on embarkation day for an appointment time that suits you. 

Fitness Center

Access to the fitness center is included in your cruise fare and it’s professionally staffed, with weight and aerobic conditioning equipment. There are also fitness classes, a sauna and steam rooms available (charges apply). Guests using the fitness center must be 16 years or over.

The Dome

If you feel like dancing the night away The Dome is the place to be. Our DJs know just how to get the party started so make sure you wear the right shoes to burn a hole in the dance floor.

Jalesh Edge

The largest adventure program at sea offering a cargo net race, slack-line bouncing bridge, high ropes, funnel climb, bungee tramp, and more!

Big Screen

Don’t want to miss out on the all-time favourite blockbuster movie? Check out our big screen movie selection. We have daily showings of the much-loved film releases across a wide variety of genres. Matinee, evening and late-night shows are available on the high seas. Come for the movies, stay for the popcorn.

Scouts

Kid's Club for 3-6 year olds, offering a LEGO zone, movies and cartoons, talent shows, theme nights, and mini Olympics. 

Adventurers

Kid's club for 7-10 year olds. Offering a LEGO zone, arts and crafts, scavenger hunts, jewelry making, air hockey, and karaoke.
