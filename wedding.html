WEDDING

A wedding may be a day’s event, but it’s a promise to hold each other for life. And taking this promise right between the blue sky and crystal sea is a memory that will last forever. Come and plan your destination wedding aboard.

FOR THE OCCASION CALLED ‘MARRIAGE’

Marriages are made in heaven, executed on land, and celebrated on Sea. Let the sea witness your memorable moment where you take vows to stay together forever. Call up your families & friends to join one of the most happening & entertaining events that are garnered with your love and friendliness. Don’t get spoilt for choices as we present you countless venue options where you can tie the knot and finally become ONE.
It’s your wedding and we’ll make sure you and your guests are pampered with choices be it for exotic cuisines, stateroom décors, leisure activities or customised function arrangements. You name it, we have it. To plan your special event, just give us a call.

ADD SOME SPLASH TO YOUR CELEBRATION

Spend some quality time with your friends & close buddies at Casino before you get hitched with your soul mate. Or pamper your better half with amazing tax & duty-free shopping experience or gift your special one from the countless designs of luxury jewellery at Pandora.
Pick from a plethora of venue options for your function, be it Mehendi, Haldi, Bachelors Party, Spinsters Party, Reception, Pre-wedding celebration or Post-wedding carnival. You name it; we’ll arrange it for you. We are just a call way!

TRADITIONAL FUNCTIONS & MORE

Every function attached to marriage is always special. Those minute details of perfection, those fragrances of Mehendi & Haldi and the joy of celebrating with close friends & families will stay in your heart for life.
Call us to plan your occasion on the cruise as per your tradition and we’ll make sure nothing is missed out because your special day is our special day too!

DÉCOR & OTHER SERVICES

As we know the ambience of the marriage is equally important as the relationship itself. Our specialized Wedding Planners on the cruise will make sure everything will be executed the exact same way you desired. Be it decorating the Holy Mandap or garnishing the entire stage with floral decorations or welcoming your guests with authentic Indian hospitality. Book our in-house musicians & singers to make your grand sangeet even grander. Don’t forget to pre-book our wedding photographers, cinematographers, make-up artists, hair stylists, spa and other services because for your every demand, we are at your service.

Some ‘We’ time

The beautiful concept of marriage drives you to a beautiful fantasy where you are under the bright blue sky, surrounded by water, cool breeze blowing, dim lighting and just two of you. We make your dream come true the exact same way that you dreamt of.
If you wish to spend some time with your special one, we have all the arrangements to make your date more romantic. Walk in to the huge Oasis overlooking the sea and watch the sun set together with no one to disturb you, except the sound of the waves. Call us to plan your special date.

The 7 Vows

Marriage in itself is a beautiful ceremony that’s close to the heart of not only the bride & groom but also to both the families. Any Hindu marriage is incomplete without the pavitra saath phere (sacred 7 vows) and we’ll make sure we adorn your perfect day as the perfect day. While the couple pledges their commitment to take care of each other in any circumstances throughout their life, we make a commitment to make this special moment a memorable one. Call our advisor to plan your perfect marriage well in advance.
