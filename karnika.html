KARNIKA

Karnika exemplifies our dream to make the world a happy place. Karnika, our maiden ship is set to take you on an unparalleled journey filled with numerous experiences that will leave you breathless and yet wanting for more. The very moment this deluxe cruise moves, cutting through the high seas, you can get high on exhilaration. Shake a leg to pulsating music, dig into tantalizing cuisines, partake in adventures, get spellbound by bespoke performances and a host of other activities. No matter what’s your age, you will still feel young at heart as the tornado of excitement hits you and fills your hearts with joy, delight and happiness. Come aboard to experience a euphoria you can never shake off.

accommodation

A Room for Everyone

Wake up to the beauty of the sea, sand and everything in between. Walk into the huge, spacious & luxury rooms that are designed to make your leisure stay at Karnika even more rich & premium.

Cruising For All

We have always believed that discovering & exploring the world around us is something that everyone should be able to enjoy. Jalesh cruise is designed keeping in mind to deliver comfort to everyone. Wheelchair-accessible rooms include wider doorways, no thresholds into bath and more handrails around the bathroom.

Adventure

If you are up for some extra adventure, join the adrenaline junkies at Jalesh Edge Adventure Park.

Food

Whether it’s relishing on plush fine dining restaurants or munching on street food delicacies, Karnika is here to please you all. Enjoy scrumptious menu for breakfast, lunch & dinner crafted by the finest chefs. What’s more? We serve authentic Jain delicacies & exotic international cuisines.

Casino

Get ready to experience a slice of Vegas right at sea. From Blackjack, Poker, Scratch-off, Slot-machines to hosts of amazing table games, the Royal Casino is sure to spoil you for choice.

Customised Celebrations

Celebrate your special occasion right in the middle of the sea, literally. Be it a birthday party, family function, friend’s reunion, anniversary or even your big fat Indian wedding, we have exclusive arrangements for all.

Shore Excursions

It’s time to get away & explore

Dive into amazing destinations to explore shore excursions that are designed exclusively for adventure junkies, foodies, shopaholics, teenagers, culture seekers, soul-searchers, peace finders, etc. Well, it’s for anybody & everybody.
