THE WATERFRONT

Savour our signature Indian Thali for lunch specially curated menu for lunch and relish on a more comprehensive menu at dinner. Thali offers the best of Indian Vegetarian & Non Vegetarian dishes served during Lunch & Dinner.

* A la Carte Menu available for Breakfast & Dinner.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch (At Sea)/Dinner
SEATING: Open with a dedicated area for Members of the Chairman’s Club & Platinum Club

CHEF’S TABLE

A Chef infused experience like no other awaits you with 3 set Menus and a choice of multiple courses featuring an Indian Banquet.

Vegetarian & Non Vegetarian, Sea Food Banquet & the traditional Dastarkhwa are available.

OPEN: Dinner
SEATING: Ceremonial Dining
Cover Charge Applies/10 Guests

CHOPSTIX

Take your taste buds on a culinary tour as we bring the best of Asian food all at one place.

Thai, Malay, Chinese, Korean, Mangolian, Taiwanese & Japanese are offered.

OPEN: Dinner
Available to Members of the Chairman’s Club for Breakfast & Lunch
SEATING: Open/Booking Recommended
Cover Charge Applies

ESSENCE OF INDIA

From Kashmir to Kanyakumari, Mumbai to Kolkata, we have explored the length and breadth of India to bring you the best of best from every region served on your plate.

This food station also doubles up as a cereal & fruit junction at breakfast.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner
SEATING: Open/Counter Service

THE FAR EASTERN KADHAI

Devour on selection of stir fry dishes created using traditional woks that breathe energy into a host of far eastern delicacies with a strong aroma and concentrated flavours.

You can also enjoy hot Indian food for breakfast.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner
SEATING: Open/Counter Service

KETTLE & BUN

For the times when you seek nothing but simple comfort food that remind you of home sweet home, make it to Kettle & Bun where we serve an extensive variety of hot & cold soups and delectable sandwiches and kebab rolls.

The station also turns into a counter serving breads, preserves and porridge for breakfast.

*Open for Evening Tea & Sandwiches

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner
SEATING: Open/Counter Service

JAIN HAVEN

The dedicated Jain Cuisine service whips up an array of authentic Jain dishes for Breakfast/Lunch & Dinner.

There’s also a special Pav Bhaji counter for those who want to indulge in lip-smacking delicacies.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner
SEATING: Open/Counter Service

VEGETARIAN BANG

Vegetarians can look forward to a wholesome selection of dishes handpicked from every part of India. Recipes that have been traditionally delighting the nation have been recreated by our expert chefs.

The station also serves a variety of fruits & cereals for breakfast.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner
SEATING: Open/Counter service

SUGAR & SPICE

If you have a sweet tooth, rest assured that we will treat you to all things sweet and nice. From classic Indian Sweets to specialty cakes, pastries, chocolates and confectioneries from around the world, find something that satiates your sugar rush.

The station also serves pastries for breakfast and tea cakes in the afternoon.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch /Dinner
SEATING: Open/Counter Service

THE INTERNATIONAL GRILL

Indulge in the comfort of simple homemade pastas. Served all day long, this station is a must experience.

Dig into a selection of Gourmet chicken, fish, vegetable burgers, pizzas & pastas.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner/Late Night Snacks
SEATING: Open/Self-service

HOT CLAY TANDOOR

The barbecue equivalent of Asia & Middle East, Hot Clay Tandoor takes you through a gastronomical journey like no other with an extensive menu of kebabs, grilled meat rolls and exotic breads.

The station doubles up as a breakfast counter for traditional hot Indian dishes.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner
SEATING: Open/Counter Service

STREET FOOD @ KARNIKA

From Khaman Dhokla from the coast of Gujarat to the fiery Mumbai Vadapav and Pav Bhaji. From Delhi Chaat to Kolkata Rolls. From Goan Omelette Pav to Chennai Idli & Dosa….everything you crave for from the Indian Street can be enjoyed at the high seas.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner
SEATING: Open/Counter Service

THE CAFE

While we know the way to your hearts through good food, we also know how your prefer your teas & coffees. The Café is your adda to enjoy cup of teas coffees all made to your discerning taste and preferences. The station also serves a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages along with exciting range of finger food and short eats.

OPEN: Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner
SEATING: Open/Counter & Waiter Service
All items are individually purchased, including finger food & short eats.
Chocolates & confectioneries for sale.

FROZEN

Your quintessential ice cream & kulfi station offers a variety of cups, cones, sundaes, traditional kulfis and our signature masala chai ice cream.

OPEN: During the day
SEATING: Open/Counter Service
All items are individually purchased
