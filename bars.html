bars

You are sure to get spoilt with choices of Bars in the high seas. Indulge in gourmet food, luxury ambience & gorgeous interiors in the middle of the vast ocean while sipping your favourite drink.

  • THE DOME
  • THE POOL BAR
  • THE OASIS BAR
  • THE CASINO BAR
  • THE MARQUEE BAR & SHOW LOUNGE
  • CONNEXIONS BARS
  • THE CHAIRMAN’S CLUB
  • MIX BAR
  • CHARLIE’S BAR

THE DOME

The late-night bar where you can reserve your table, The Dome offers the finest selection of alcohol and your much needed private space to enjoy a drink.

LOCATION: Deck 14
OPEN: Evening till late night

THE POOL BAR

Sip from a wide selection of freshly made fruit juices and a wide variety of mocktails, cocktails and drinks. Just the perfect place to be in company of family and friends enjoying your favourite sundowners.

LOCATION: Deck 12
OPEN: Late Morning/Afternoon/Evening (Sundowner)

THE OASIS BAR

Open for members, during the day and for all guests (Adults Only) in the evenings, The Oasis Bar is the place to enjoy the best of traditional Hookahs and Cigars along with a fine selection of speciality cognacs and single malts.

LOCATION: Deck 10
OPEN: Afternoon/Evening

THE CASINO BAR

Enjoy all the action at the Casino with your favourite drinks served at the players and spectators table. Experience a slice of Vegas right at the sea with thousands of square feet of lights, action, slots and drinks.

LOCATION: Deck 8
OPEN: Evening/Late Night

THE MARQUEE BAR & SHOW LOUNGE

The theatre style bar where you can enjoy your favourite musical, entertainment show or movie along with a wide choice of drinks.

LOCATION: Deck 7
OPEN: Evening/Late Night (During show times)

CONNEXIONS BARS

As the name suggests this is just the place to enjoy with family and new friends made onboard. Great choice of drinks & beverages, and happy music is all you need to connect.

LOCATION: Deck 7
OPEN: (Sea Days Open Earlier) Evening

THE CHAIRMAN’S CLUB

Reserved for Platinum Members & Members of Chairman’s Club, this is an exclusive place to enjoy the best of signature beverages from around the world.

LOCATION: Deck 7
OPEN: During The Day/Evening/Late Night

MIX BAR

Go around the world in a few tipples as we serve you the best of Craft Beer, Bottled Beers, Gins & Whiskeys from all over the world.

LOCATION: Deck 6 &7
OPEN: Evening/Late Night

CHARLIE’S BAR

The speciality Barista serving signature Coffee & Chai along with indulging choice of food.

LOCATION: Deck 5
OPEN: All day and till evening
All items are individually purchased.
Chocolates and confectioneries are for sale.
