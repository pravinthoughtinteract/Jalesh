Experience The Glory of Goa

The effervescent state of pompous parties and historical heritage, Goa is one of the most sought-after tourist destinations in India. Known for its spirited nightlife and leisure activities, this tropical paradise also offers you vibrant vistas and moments to cherish for a lifetime.

Crocodile & Sacred Monuments

On this odyssey of experiencing nature and discovering yourself, you get to explore the picturesque backwaters of Goa. Wade through the scintillating backwaters catching the sight of river crocodiles & tropical bird species alongside the banks of River Zuari. This marine life rich ecosystem also sustains a healthy population of exotic animals and bird species. Furthermore, your tour also allows you to delve into the magnificence of Se Cathedral - the seat of the Portuguese Bishop and one of the largest Churches in Asia, as well as the Basilica of Bom Jesus, where the sacred relics of the Spanish Saint Francis Xavier are venerated, ensuring an enlightening day of contrasting experiences.

Old Goa, Fontainhas & Panjim

Embark upon a journey of spirituality through the rustic villages of Goa that lead you to the mighty Mangueshi temple. Following which, you get to experience inspiring architectures of Se Cathedral - one of Asia’s biggest churches along with Basilica of Bom Jesus. To complement all this, a drive to Panjim lets you explore Portuguese heritage best observed by walking through the quaint Latin Quarter with its narrow streets, old Mansions with overhanging balconies, red tiled roofs and numerous Restaurants, Bars and Cafe’s. Capture the colorful insights of the Latin Quarter at Panjim, while you shop through the streets bustling with souvenirs, handicrafts, Tibetan goods and much more.

Island & Dolphin Fantasy

There is scarcely a placid sight than watching the elegance of dolphins in their natural habitat alongside other marine flora & fauna of the Grande Island. Your itinerary is packed with fun-filled activities like adventure boat sailing, snorkelling & fishing in the clear waters of the Island. Although, we think that beach barbeque with exotic seafood is going to be the highlight of your trip.

Spiritual Insights of Goa

Immerse in the vast spiritual heritage & revered ancient architecture that the state has to offer. Explore the grandeur of St. Cajetan Church, built on the model of St. Peter in Rome that fascinates you with its characteristic architectural style being the only domed church remaining in Goa. Your tour also features a visit to the Shantadurga Temple, followed by the Safa Masjid - the largest and most famous mosque in Goa. Built in 1560 by Ibrahim Adilshah of Bijapur, the mosque is flanked by a skilfully-built masonry tank revealing small chambers with Mihrab designs.

Flavours of Goa

With the mythological significance of being a place where Shiva set foot on, Mangueshi Temple is Goa’s most frequented temple, visited by a large number of locals, domestic and foreign tourists. The temple architecture portrays a unique blend of various influences prevalent in the times of yore. With a tour of the local spice plantation that tends to enrich your senses, you also get to learn how local spices and herbs are grown organically. Interspersed in the plantation are also fruit trees and vegetables like cashew, custard apple, banana, citrus fruits and pineapples. Take a guided walk through the trees to identify your senses of smell and taste, and learn about the myriad of spices India is renowned for while you may even see an elephant enroute. From watching betel nut pluckers climb 100-feet trees to indulging in fresh fruits and locally prepared snacks, your trip is bound to tantalise your taste buds and your mind.
