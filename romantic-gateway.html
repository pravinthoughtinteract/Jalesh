Romantic Getaway

What is better than having a cozy dinner overlooking the sparkling ocean? Plan your dreamy moment with your soul mate, be it dancing all night or having an
intimate conversation over a candlelight dinner. Come, fall in love all over again.

Spa at Sea

Enjoy the rejuvenating treatment together at the most exotic spa onboard that’s sure to relax your senses, uplift your soul and enrich your mind. The Zen aroma will help to let loose the burden and make you feel light and refreshed.
Choose from varied therapy for your special one or engage for couple massage & relaxing therapy. We have well-trained professional therapists who will keep your mind at rest, body at peace and your soul cheerful. The Spa is open daily so you can book an appointment at a time that suits you the best.

The Oasis

Cocktail in hand, viewing the sun setting over the ship’s wake and your special one to accompany you, isn’t it just the perfect romantic date on the cruise? Come aboard to The Oasis and enjoy quality time at the specially designed cushioned cabanas and witness the peaceful haven where the waves of the sea are the only one to disturb you.
The Oasis is a child-free zone to let the adults escape into an aura of love & happiness.

The Waterfront

Make your special day really special. Whether it's popping the question to your beloved, celebrating a milestone anniversary or birthday, or just reaffirming your vows, experience of doing so on board a cruise ship is priceless. With Ala carte menu available for dinner, this restaurant serves their signature Indian Thali feasting on a specially curated menu that offers the best of Indian Vegetarian & Non Vegetarian dishes served during Lunch & Dinner.

CHOPSTIX

Take your taste buds on a culinary tour as we bring the best of Thai, Malay, Chinese, Korean, Mangolian, Taiwanese & Japanese delicacies which will be served all at one place under a specific charge.
