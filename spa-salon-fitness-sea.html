Spa, Salon & Fitness at Sea

Relaxing your mind, body & soul on a cruise is the best gift you can give yourself. Try our exotic spa therapy from professional therapists to wake-up your senses and refresh your soul. If you are looking out to stay fit even onboard, we have a fully-equipped gym with trainers to supervise your fitness regime. If you choose to transform your look and decide for a makeover, hit straight to our uber salon and we are sure you’ll love it. Step-in to Deck 14 to experience all of these.

Serana Spa

With the breeze of the ocean, the rich zen aroma and gorgeous natural textures, you are sure to have a tranquil spa experience on the cruise. The Spa opens up early in the morning and closes late at night, so get the privilege to choose your time slot as per your convenience.

  • Body Massage
  • Body Therapy
  • Thermal Suite
  • Anti-Ageing Treatment
  • Skin Solutions
  • Acupuncture Session
  • Medi-Spa Cosmetic Treatments

Salon

Choose from numerous beauty, hair & body treatments from haircutting, hairstyling, extensions, waxing, massages, hair colouring and lot more. Book our expert stylists in advance if you wish to plan your wedding on the cruise.

  • Shave Bar
  • Face Bar
  • Blow Out Bar
  • Nail Bar
  • Hair Bar
  • Colour Bar
  • Wax Bar

Fitness

Power up with the ultimate fitness experience! A mix of fun, exercise & training along with nutrition consultation and specialized classes is what makes us stand apart from the others. Stay fit even on the high sea.
