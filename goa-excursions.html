Exprience The Glory of Goa

The effervescent state of pompous parties and historical heritage, Goa is one of the most sought-after tourist destinations in India. Known for its spirited nightlife and leisure activities, this tropical paradise also offers you vibrant vistas and moments to cherish for a lifetime.

Crocodile & Sacred Monuments

Explore the scintillating backwaters of Goa that house river crocodiles & tropical bird species alongside the banks of River Zuari. Furthermore, your tour also allows you to delve into the magnificence of Se Cathedral as well as Basilica of Bom Jesus.

Heritage Trail Varga House

Experience Goa’s colonial past amidst the tranquil environs at Braganza Mansion and the 213-year old Palácio do Deão. To add more flavour, you get treated to delicious & traditional Goan meals at a hamlet that feels like home.

Island & Dolphin Fantasy

There is scarcely a placid sight than watching the elegance of dolphins in their natural habitat alongside other marine flora & fauna of the Grande Island. Packed with fun-filled activities like snorkelling & fishing, we think that beach barbeque with exotic seafood is going to be the highlight of your trip.

Flea Market Noche De Carnival De Goa

Not just another flea market, it’s an experience that can be availed only on Wednesdays. What initially was set up by hippies exchanging their belongings for money, is now a major tourist attraction for souvenirs, handicrafts, Tibetan goods and much more.

South Goa - Kayaking Adventure

If adventure is your way of retreat then we have just the exact sojourn for you. Brave through the unexplored tropics of Goa as you wade through the tranquillity of nature, in your own kayak amidst the surrealism of sunrise & sunset.

Spiritual Insights of Goa

Immerse in the vast spiritual heritage that the state has to offer. Right from the grandeur of St. Cajetan Church to the mighty Shantadurga Temple to the majesty of Safa Masjid, your trip is packed with revered ancient architecture and the deep-rooted culture of Goa.

Flavours of Goa

The architectural splendour of Mangueshi temple followed by visiting the hub of Goa’s organic spice plantation forms a small part of the essence of Goa. From watching betel nut pluckers climb 100-feet trees to indulging in fresh fruits and locally prepared snacks, your trip is bound to tantalise your taste buds and your mind.

The Dudhsagar Waterfalls

Known as Goa’s captivating cascade, you get to experience the majesty of nature that rests amidst the wildlife sanctuary of Bhagwan Mahavir. Following the towering 600m-view of pure bliss, is an elephant ride that just cannot be missed. Traditional Goan Lunch on your way back, and a scenic drive only add to your day’s delight.
